No-Refund Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025
Important Notice: All CarLedgr subscription sales are final. We do not provide refunds for any reason.

15-Day Free Trial

All CarLedgr subscriptions include a 15-day free trial period. During this trial:

  • You have full access to all features of your selected plan
  • You can cancel at any time without charge
  • No payment is required until the trial period ends
  • If you cancel during the trial, you will not be charged

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your CarLedgr subscription at any time:

  • Self-Service: Cancel through your account dashboard
  • Email: Contact us at support@carledgr.com
  • Immediate Effect: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • Data Access: You retain access to your data until the subscription period ends

No-Refund Policy

All sales are final. CarLedgr does not provide refunds for subscriptions under any circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Change of mind after subscribing
  • Failure to use the service
  • Technical difficulties on your end
  • User error or misuse of the platform
  • Dissatisfaction with features or functionality
  • Business closure or changes
  • Service interruptions or downtime
  • Data loss (see our Terms of Service for limitations)

Billing Error Resolution

The only exception to our no-refund policy is for genuine billing errors caused by our system, such as:

  • Duplicate charges for the same subscription period
  • Charges after successful cancellation during trial period
  • Technical errors in our payment processing

To report a billing error, contact support@carledgr.com within 30 days of the charge with documentation of the error.

Enterprise Customers

Customers with custom enterprise agreements may have different refund terms as specified in their individual contracts. Please refer to your signed agreement or contact your account manager.

Contact for Refund Requests

To request a refund:

  • Email: support@carledgr.com
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Organization Name]"
  • Include: Organization name, billing email, reason for refund

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 24 hours.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notification to active subscribers
  • In-app notification in your CarLedgr dashboard
  • Updated "Last modified" date on this page

Questions? Our support team is here to help clarify any aspect of our refund policy. Don't hesitate to reach out at support@carledgr.com.
