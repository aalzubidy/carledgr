Comprehensive dealership management features

Purpose-built for vehicle repair and resale business operations

Complete Inventory Management

Track vehicles through their entire lifecycle from acquisition to sale. Know exactly what's in stock, under repair, or sold with VIN-based search and auto-population.

Detailed Repair Tracking

Log every maintenance record - oil changes, brake replacements, body work. Each repair automatically adds to vehicle cost with receipt attachments and categorization.

Three Powerful Report Types

Generate Inventory reports, Maintenance reports, and Profit reports with flexible date range filters.

Role-Based Access Control

Three user roles: Owner, Manager, and Operator. Secure team collaboration with proper permissions.

Smart Attachments & Backup

Attach receipts, photos, and PDFs to every maintenance record and expense. All data automatically backed up and replicated - reduce the risk of losing your records.

Multi-Language & User-Friendly

Available in English, Spanish, Arabic, and French. Intuitive interface with VIN auto-population, smart search by vin/make/model/color, and exceptional user experience design.
