Getting Started

Welcome to CarLedgr

Logging In

To access CarLedgr, you'll need:

  • Organization Name: Your dealership's organization name
  • Email: Your registered email address
  • Password: Your account password
Language Selection: You can change the interface language using the globe icon (üåê) in the bottom right corner of any page.

Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your dealership's performance with real-time metrics and insights.

Inventory Metrics

  • Total Cars: Complete count of all vehicles in your system
  • In Stock: Vehicles currently available for sale
  • Sold: Successfully sold vehicles
  • Pending: Vehicles in process or under evaluation
  • Current Inventory Value: Total value of unsold inventory

Financial Overview

  • Total Profit: Overall profit across all sold vehicles
  • Net Profit: Profit after all expenses
  • Profit This Month: Current month's profit performance
  • Cars Sold This Month: Number of vehicles sold in current month

Performance Analytics

  • Top Sold Models: Most sold vehicle types in the system
  • Total Revenue: Total revenue per vehicle brand and model
  • Average Sale Price: The average sale price per vehicle models for the top sold models

Favorite Cars

The dashboard displays a dedicated section for your favorite cars, providing quick access to important vehicles:

  • Quick Access: Click on any favorite car to navigate directly to its details page
  • Real-time Updates: Favorite cars list updates automatically when vehicles are added or removed from favorites
  • Empty State: When no cars are favorited, helpful guidance is provided on how to add vehicles to favorites

Car Management

The Cars section is where you manage your entire vehicle inventory from acquisition to sale.

Adding a New Car

  1. Click the "Add Car" button
  2. Enter the VIN number - the system will automatically populate make, model, and year
  3. Fill in additional details:
    • Color
    • Mileage
    • Purchase date and price
    • Current status (defaults to "Pending" for new vehicles)
  4. Click "Save" to add the vehicle
VIN Auto-Population: When you enter a VIN, CarLedgr automatically fetches vehicle information to save you time and ensure accuracy.

Vehicle Statuses

Status Description Use Case
Pending Vehicle is awaiting initial processing Recently acquired, waiting on paperwork, or initial evaluation
In Repair Vehicle is actively being worked on Under repair, restoration, or maintenance work
In Stock Vehicle is available for sale Completed repairs, ready for customer purchase
Sold Vehicle has been sold Transaction completed, no longer in active inventory

Searching and Filtering

You can search for vehicles using:

  • VIN number - Full or partial VIN
  • Make and Model - Vehicle brand and model
  • Year - Manufacturing year
  • Color - Vehicle color
  • Status - Current vehicle status

Editing Vehicle Information

  1. Click the "Edit" button next to any vehicle
  2. Modify the necessary information
  3. Update sale information when a vehicle is sold
  4. Click "Save" to update the record

Vehicle Details Page

Click on any vehicle row to view comprehensive details including:

  • Complete vehicle specifications
  • Purchase and sale history
  • All maintenance records
  • Total costs and profit calculations
  • Attached documents and receipts for the maintenance records

Print Option: Vehicle details pages include a print button to generate hard copies of individual car information and history.

Favorite Cars

Mark important vehicles as favorites for quick access and monitoring:

  • Adding to Favorites: On any vehicle details page, click the "Add to Favorites" button to mark the car as a favorite
  • Removing from Favorites: Click the "Favorited" button on the vehicle details page to remove it from your favorites
  • Dashboard View: All your favorite cars are displayed in a dedicated section on the main dashboard for quick access
  • Limit: You can have up to 10 favorite cars at a time
Pro Tip: Use favorites to track high-priority vehicles, cars with ongoing issues, or your most profitable inventory items.

Maintenance Tracking

The Maintenance section allows you to track all repairs, services, and improvements performed on your vehicles.

Adding Maintenance Records

  1. Click "Add Maintenance"
  2. Search and select the vehicle (by VIN, make, model, or year)
  3. Choose a maintenance category (If you need a new category, only the owner of the organization can create it)
  4. Enter the description and cost
  5. Set the maintenance date
  6. Add vendor information (optional)
  7. Include any additional notes (optional)
  8. Click "Save" to record the maintenance

Attaching Documents

For each maintenance record, you can add an attachment. The accepted formats are PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP and the maximum file size is 8MB.

Automatic Cost Calculation: All maintenance costs are automatically added to the vehicle's total cost, helping you track true profitability.

Searching Maintenance Records

You can search maintenance records by:

  • Vehicle VIN, make, or model
  • Maintenance description
  • Category type
  • Vendor name
  • Cost amount

Editing and Deleting Records

  • Edit: Click the edit button to modify any maintenance record
  • Delete: Removes the maintenance record from the system permanently
  • View Car: Jump directly to the vehicle's detail page

Dealership Expenses

Track your dealership's operational expenses separate from vehicle-specific maintenance costs.

Access Level: Only Owners and Managers can access the Expenses section.

Types of Dealership Expenses

CarLedgr includes predefined expense categories specifically designed for car dealerships: Rent, Utilities, Insurance, Office Supplies, Advertising & Marketing, Fuel, and Other. These default categories provide a solid foundation for tracking common dealership expenses. Organization owners can also create additional custom categories to match your specific business needs.

Adding Expense Records

  1. Click "Add Expense"
  2. Select or create an expense category (If you need a new category, only the owner of the organization can create it)
  3. Enter the expense amount
  4. Add a description
  5. Set the expense date
  6. Attach receipts or invoices (optional) - accepted formats are PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP with maximum file size of 8MB
  7. Click "Save"

Filtering Expenses

You can filter your expenses by:

  • Category: View expenses for specific categories or all together
  • Date Range: Filter expenses within a specific time period
  • Combined Filters: Use both category and date filters together for precise results

Expense Summary

View important expense metrics:

  • Total Amount: Sum of all recorded expenses
  • Total Count (Available at the bottom of the table): Number of expense records
  • Category Breakdown: Expenses by category
  • Monthly Breakdown: Expenses by month

Print Option: Expense summaries and detailed records can be printed using the print button available on the expenses page.

Tax Benefits

Hint! Tax Preparation: Detailed expense tracking helps you maximize business deductions and simplifies tax preparation at year-end.

Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports to analyze your dealership's performance and profitability.

Report Types

1. Inventory Reports

Get insights into your current inventory status:

  • Inventory Status: Breakdown by In Stock, Sold, Pending, and In Repair
  • Inventory Value: Total value of unsold inventory
  • Vehicle Distribution: List of vehicles within the specified time period and their purchase price, total maintenance costs, and total invetment (purchase price + maintenance costs)

2. Maintenance Reports

Analyze maintenance costs and patterns:

  • Total Maintenance Costs: Overall spending on repairs
  • All Maintenance Records: Total number of repair records
  • Total Car Costs: The value of the cars in the inventory (without maintenance costs)
  • Total Investment: The total of the cars in the inventory (purchase price + maintenance costs)
  • Total Cars: Total number of cars in the filtered dataset
  • Cars In Repair: Number of cars currently under repair or maintenance work
  • Average Cost per Car: The average maintenance cost per car
  • Models Breakdown: Total and average costs by car model
  • Category Breakdown: Total and average costs by maintenance type
  • Vehicle Information: List of vehicles in that specific time period and the total number of maintenance records and the total cost of maintenance

3. Sales/Profit Reports

Track your financial performance:

  • Total Revenue: Income from vehicle sales
  • Total Costs: Purchase prices plus maintenance
  • Net Profit: Revenue minus all costs
  • Total Sales: Total number of vehicles sold
  • Total Maintenance cost: Total maintenance cost for all vehicles sold
  • Monthly Breakdown: Total revenue, total number of sales, and net profit by month
  • Make Breakdown: Total revenue, total number of sales, and net profit by car make
  • Vehicle Information: List of vehicles in that specific time period and the cost and profit information

Date Range Filtering

All reports support flexible date filtering:

  • Today: Current day only
  • This Week: Current week's data
  • This Month: Current month's data
  • This Year: Current year's data
  • Custom Range: Select specific start and end dates
  • All Time: Complete historical data

Category Filtering

For maintenance reports, you can filter by specific maintenance categories or view all categories together for comprehensive analysis.

Printing and Exporting Reports

CarLedgr provides multiple options for sharing and saving your reports:

  • Print Reports: All report types include print buttons for generating hard copies
  • Export Reports: Reports can be exported for external analysis or sharing with accountants and business partners

Settings & Configuration

Access Level: Only Owners can access the Settings section.

License Usage

Monitor your subscription plan usage:

  • Current Plan: Your active subscription tier
  • Active Cars: Number of vehicles currently in your inventory in all statuses except sold
  • Car Limit: Maximum allowed vehicles in your plan. Remember this is for cars in all statuses except sold for your plan (You can upgrade your plan to increase the limit)
  • Usage Percentage: How much of your limit is being used

Category Management System

CarLedgr uses a two-tier category system for both expenses and maintenance:

Default Categories

  • System-Provided: Pre-configured categories designed specifically for car dealerships
  • Read-Only: Default categories cannot be edited or deleted to ensure consistency
  • Always Available: Default categories are available to all organizations
  • Visual Indicator: Marked with a "Default Category" badge in the settings table

Custom Categories

  • Organization-Specific: Create categories unique to your business needs
  • Full Control: Custom categories can be edited, renamed, or deleted
  • Flexible Management: Move records between categories before deletion
  • Visual Indicator: Marked with a "Custom Category" badge in the settings table

Expense Categories

Default expense categories include: Rent, Utilities, Insurance, Office Supplies, Advertising & Marketing, Fuel, and Other. Owners can create additional custom categories as needed.

Maintenance Categories

Comprehensive default maintenance categories cover all common repair types including Engine, Transmission, Brakes, Electrical, Body, Tires, Oil Change, Diagnostic, Detailing, Battery, Fuel System, Cooling System, Pre-Sale Inspection, and various taxes and fees. Owners can add custom categories for specialized services.

Category Table Features

  • Category Type Column: Clearly shows whether each category is "Default" or "Custom"
  • Usage Tracking: Displays the number of records in each category
  • Smart Actions: Edit and delete buttons are automatically disabled for default categories
  • Search & Filter: Search by category name, type (default/custom), or usage frequency
  • Sorting: Sort by name, type, or usage count

User Management

Owners can manage team members and their access:

  • Add Users: Add other users to your organization with appropriate roles
  • Edit Users: Update user information and assign or update their roles
  • Delete Users: Remove team members from the organization
  • Role Assignment: Set Owner, Manager, or Operator access levels
  • Account Restrictions: Owners cannot delete or reassign their own account. Please contact support@carledgr.com if you need to delete or reassign your own account.

Profile Management

All users can update their personal account information:

  • Name: First and last name
  • Email: Contact email address
  • Password: Account password
  • Language: Interface language preference
Access: Profile management is available to all users through the user dropdown menu in the top right corner.

Common Questions

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your organization name and email to receive reset instructions.

Can I export my data?

Yes, reports can be exported for external analysis. Contact support for bulk data export options.

How many users can I have?

User limits depend on your subscription plan. Check your Settings page for current limits.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted, automatically backed up, and stored securely. Each organization's data is completely isolated.

Can I customize categories?

Yes, CarLedgr uses a two-tier category system. Default categories are provided for common dealership needs and cannot be modified to ensure consistency. Owners can create additional custom categories for specialized business needs in the Settings section. Both default and custom categories are clearly marked in the category management tables.

Tip: Most questions can be answered by exploring the relevant section of this documentation or by contacting your organization's Owner or Manager.
